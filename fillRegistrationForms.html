Fill Registration Form

  1. Make sure "Bookmarks bar" is displayed.
  2. Drag the "Fill Registration Form" button to your Bookmark bar
  3. On the registration page, click on the "Fill Registration Form" bookmark. Now, you can see most of the fields filled.

Once installed on your browser, the  Fill Registration Form  button lets you fill the most of the fields in the registration form of Free/Wager play websites like GNNJ, Cotriga etc.,
